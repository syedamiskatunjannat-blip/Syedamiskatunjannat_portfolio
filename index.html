Research

Molecular Dynamics Study of Functionally Graded Al–Cu Nanowire

Undergraduate Thesis · April 2025 — Present
Advisor: Prof. Dr. Muhammed Mahbubur Razzaque, Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, BUET

  • Performing molecular dynamics simulations in LAMMPS to study functionally graded Al–Cu nanowires.
  • Investigating the effect of different grading functions and Cu content variation on mechanical properties, including tensile and compressive strength.
  • Analyzing dislocation behaviors and deformation mechanisms under varying grading conditions.
  • Working toward structure–property correlations to inform the design of advanced nanowire materials.
